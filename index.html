What is a LUG?

LUG is short for LEGO User Group. LUGs are groups of LEGO fans that get together to geek out about the brick, share information, show off their builds, and organize for events.

What is SandLUG?

What is SandLUG?

SandLUG is a LUG based in San Diego aimed at adult fans of LEGO (AFOLs). We hold meetings on the 3rd Saturday of each month and organize several display events throughout the year. We coordinate our activities online via a Google group, and info on how to join is below.

Younger members are welcome, but you will need your parents to stay with you at any meetings or events you wish to attend (for pesky liability reasons).

How do I join?

Joining is Easy!

We use a Google Groups forum to organize SandLUG events and activities. Simply go to the following link while logged in with a Google account to apply for membership (please take a moment to fill out the questionnaire so we know a bit about you when you join!): https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/sandlug

We look forward to meeting you!
